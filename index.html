Welcome to Saudamini Lokyaan Company Pvt Ltd, your premier partner for electric vehicle solutions. We specialize in the import, distribution, and servicing of various types of electric vehicles and related infrastructure.

Our mission is to promote environment-friendly transportation by providing cutting-edge EV technology, comprehensive charging solutions, and exceptional after-sales support across local markets.

Our Mission

To revolutionize transportation in Nepal by providing cutting-edge electric vehicle solutions, comprehensive charging infrastructure, and exceptional services that promote sustainable mobility and reduce environmental impact.

To be Nepal's leading electric mobility solutions provider, driving the transition to zero-emission transportation through innovative technologies, nationwide infrastructure, and eco-friendly transportation alternatives.
